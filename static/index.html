Client 1 represents a primary enterprise client node. In our log classification architecture, clients securely forward diagnostic logs generated from separate local source subsystems such as CRM apps or Billing platforms.

Logs from Client 1 are aggregated and compiled with standard timezone timestamps and origin signatures before being forwarded to the primary central buffer.
